The Key Insight

Controls fail at 4pm on Friday, not during audits

Separation of duties doesn't fail during audits. It fails at 4pm on Friday when the approver is traveling, at month-end when volume doubles, and when the CEO says "just get it done." This diagnostic tests the moments that matter.
