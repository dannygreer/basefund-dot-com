The Key Insight

Rail selection is a security decision, not a logistics one

Payment rail selection is usually a logistics decision made at the last moment. But the rail you choose determines how much verification time you have, whether you can intervene if something goes wrong, and whether recovery is possible. It should be a security decision, made early.
