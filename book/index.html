About the Book

A practical framework for securing every transaction.

Every year, organizations lose billions to transaction fraud, errors, and process breakdowns — not because they lack technology, but because they lack a framework. When Money Moves changes that.

Written by Robert White, founder of Basefund, this book distills years of experience securing high-value transactions into a repeatable, implementable system. Whether you're moving funds via wire, ACH, check, or digital rails, the Secure Transactions Framework gives you the structure to eliminate risk at every stage.

This isn't theory. Every chapter pairs real-world case studies with hands-on tools you can deploy immediately — scorecards, checklists, audit templates, and interactive diagnostics that turn the framework into action.

01 — The 1800s

In the 1800s, your banker knew your family. Trust was earned face-to-face. Money moved slowly — and that friction was its own kind of security.

02 — The Wire Age

Then came the telegraph. Then wire transfers. Then SWIFT. Each innovation solved a problem and created a new one.

03 — The Digital Era

Speed increased. Visibility decreased. We lost the ability to tell a trusted partner from someone pretending to be one.
