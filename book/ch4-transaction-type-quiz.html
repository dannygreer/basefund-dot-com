The Key Insight

Misclassification is structural risk

Misclassification isn't just a labeling error — it's a structural risk. When a one-time payment to a new vendor gets treated like a recurring payment to an established one, the wrong controls apply. The decision tree gives your team a repeatable framework for getting it right every time.
