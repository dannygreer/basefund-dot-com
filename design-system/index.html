41 · UI Patterns
Accordion — FAQ
How quickly does identity verification complete?
▾
Basefund's real-time KYB/KYC layer verifies identity against authoritative government and financial data sources in under 1 second for most entities. Complex cases involving multiple jurisdictions may take up to 30 seconds. No manual review is required unless a match cannot be confirmed automatically.
What types of transactions does Basefund support?
▾
Basefund is purpose-built for high-value institutional transactions: municipal bond proceeds, refunding transactions, capital project disbursements, real estate closings, and interbank transfers. The platform is optimized for transactions where identity, routing accuracy, and insurance are critical requirements.
How is transaction-specific insurance different from a blanket policy?
▾
Traditional financial insurance is a blanket policy covering an institution's general operations. Basefund's transaction-specific insurance is underwritten individually at deal initiation — bound to the specific amount, parties, and terms of your transaction. Coverage is active from the moment you create a deal, not retroactively applied after a loss.
What compliance frameworks does Basefund satisfy?
▾
Basefund is SOC 2 Type II certified, GLBA compliant, and FINRA-aligned. The platform's tamper-evident audit trail satisfies the record-keeping requirements of most municipal bond regulations. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated compliance documentation package for regulatory review.
