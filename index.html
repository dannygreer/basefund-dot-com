Why Basefund

The infrastructure gap in institutional finance.

High-value transactions move through a patchwork of phone calls, email threads, and manual wire instructions — with no systematic protection layer.

The Problem
Fragmented, manual, and uninsured.
Municipal bond proceeds and capital project disbursements flow through disconnected systems with no unified verification, no real-time visibility, and no protection against wire fraud, misdirection, or counterparty failure.
The Cost
$15B lost to wire fraud annually.
The FBI estimates $15B in annual losses from Business Email Compromise targeting financial transactions. Municipal issuers and institutional counterparties are prime targets — and most have no transaction-level protection in place.
The Basefund Approach
Infrastructure that moves with the money.
Basefund wraps every transaction in a verified, insured, and coordinated layer — so that by the time a wire leaves, every account has been validated, every party has been identified, and every condition has been met.
